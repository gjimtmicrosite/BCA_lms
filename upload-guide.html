💻 Student Upload & Submission Guide — BCA Sem 1

This guide explains how BCA with AI Integration students must prepare, name, organise, and upload their shadow project files for Semester 1. Every submission must follow the exact format below.

📂 File Naming SP1 Checklist SP2 Checklist GSDC Mock Tests Google Drive

📂 File Naming Convention

Every file must follow this exact format:

[UserID]_[ProjectCode]_[DocumentType].[extension]
Examples:
bca26-ai-03_SP1_AlgorithmComparison.pdf
bca26-ai-04_SP1_CProgram.c
bca26-ai-02_SP2_UtilityProgram.c
bca26-ai-09_SP2_UserManual.pdf
❌ Do NOT use spaces in filenames.
❌ Do NOT rename files after uploading.
✅ Export reports and documentation as PDF.
